of nodes and networks

The internet is supremely complex and outrageously complicated, however stripped down to the core it is simply one big network of human nodes and hardware nodes, clustering in myriad forms, all for the purpose of enabling communication and resource sharing with one another at an exponential scale.

shared interests = shared vectors

shared interests = shared vectors

My intention for this site is to establish a table of contents of sorts to the book of my internet self, where the chapters are my assorted digital efforts and the glossary is a growing list of associative activators interconnecting these efforts.

My hope is that this helps make me more accessible to others in the network I may not yet know or may not yet know me.

My motivation is allow for network effects to flow with less interference. 

The underpinning formula behind network effects and its fabled abundance relies on two simple drivers: sharing available resources willingly and effective knowledge of the available resource pool and the respective access protocols for each resource.

Its a feedback loop of magnificently virtuous alignment when done in good faith and distilled down to its essence is this: the more you share, the more you have. what’s good for someone other than yourself is disproportionately better for everyone, including yourself.

It’s the world’s ultimate cheat code: The most self interested and self-serving thing you can possibly do in the long term, is to be as selfless, empathetic and helpful as possible in the ever perpetual near term.
