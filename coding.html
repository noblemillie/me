Software Projects

I'm an explorer at heart, have forever been drawn towards the unknown and have always experienced profound meaning in the journey. 

I consider the publishing of this site as the latest chapter in a life I would consider truly blessed, even if it all ended tomorrow. My strategy through the earlier chapters of my career (professional services, business, hospitality and nature) could be aptly described as a multi-dimensional career path with a route charted through a function that has personal curiousity and knowledge domain wanderlust as its operative arguments.

 In practice, this manifests itself by developing a general systems-level  mental map for the respective knowledge domain, then working my way to the edge of the field I'm most interested in seeing first-hand. I can't image that this stage (creating software products) will be any different.  

 The following set of portfolio projects represent my inital forrays toward a particular frontier of computer science I find most personally captivating, which could be generally considered as the overlap of two subdomains: data visualisation and workflow tools whose value-added can be explicitly quantified. 

inanimatedObjects

An open-source effort to contribute useful, reusable data visualization tools.

Perkolator

An interactive dashboard for evaluating terms in a job offer

dataset.tools

A utilty for the data.world community.
