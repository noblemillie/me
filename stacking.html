Stacks on Stacks

My professional aspiration is to achieve comprehensive systems-level knowledge of business. My path toward this goal has led me to acquire first-hand experience in a number of different knowledge domains I've found to be both complementary and adjacent to one another. I'm not sure what the career path of a true full-stack employee looks like, but I'm committed to finding it. Here's my current cross-disciplinary functional knowledge:

Software

My obsession of the last few years has been with software development. I've crossed the full-stack rubicon and am pretty confident I've  embarked on a lifelong journey.

Writing

Whether it's legalese, blogging, techical documentation, event planning or code, I'm an unapologetic lover of language.

Design

At the end of the day it all comes down to style, our universal differentiator. I created a variety of content over the years, from prototyping to web design to data visualization to full stack media production (photo/ video/audio).

Financial Modeling

There was a time where Excel was pratically an appendage of mine and frozen more computers from excessive file size than I'm proud to admit. From forensic reconstruction of financial accounts to transaction modeling to valuation, it was the original swiss army knife of my professional life.
